Description

Gyoza that would wake the dead

Ingredients

  • - [ ] Pork mince
  • - [ ] Spring onions
  • - [ ] Wombok or cabbage sliced thin
  • - [ ] Soy sauce
  • - [ ] Salt and pepper
  • - [ ] Sesame oil
  • - [ ] Ginger and garlic
  • - [ ] Sake
  • - [ ] Gyoza wrappers

Instructions

  1. Mix pork mince with hand until fat starts turning white
  2. Add: spring onions, wombok, salt, peper, sesame oil, soy sauce, sake ginger & garlic
  3. Mix ingredients well. If you are not ready to do it with your hands, you should not be doing this recipie. You are not welcome here
  4. Prepare dish with water
  5. To make the dumpling, take a spoonfull of filling and place it in the middle of the gyoza wrapper.
  6. Wetten finger and moisten the edge of the wrapper
  7. Fold edges together, making sure that no air is trapped inside
  8. Make three pinches in the gyoza flap
  9. Repeat until filling or wrappers run out
  10. Add oil and gyoza onto cold pan. Heat the pan over high flame. Once sizlling flip the gyoza and add a splash of water. Cover the gyoza immedietly and steam for 4 minutes.
  11. Serve gyoza with dumpling sauce; chilly oil; seseame seads and spring onions

Hot tip: if you have left over filling, make it into a patty and pan fry it like a burger. Eat it with a fried egg over some rice for breakfast. Your tummy will thank you.
